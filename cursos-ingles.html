INTENSIVE COURSES

PREPARATORIES FOR LEVEL A1 AND A2 OF SIELE

SIELE EXAM

International Spanish Language Evaluation Service

It is promoted by the Cervantes Institute, the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), the University of Salamanca (USAL) and the University of Buenos Aires (UBA)

INTENSIVE SPANISH COURSE 40 hours - 2 WEEKS

Two levels of SIELE (A1 and A2) in groups of up to 10 students.

The objective is to improve the degree of competence in the Spanish language through reading comprehension, listening comprehension, written expression and interaction, and oral expression and interaction.

Classes are integrated with a series of activities that offer improved practices in real environments. Students interact in Spanish to improve their listening, speaking, reading and writing skills.

ENJOY OUR ACTIVITIES!

We design a set of activities so that you can get to know our culture while practicing your Spanish.

Experience the Argentine countryside first-hand, learn to cook some of our typical dishes and enjoy nights of music.

Guided tours to the mountains of Tandil and the Tapalque Hot Springs, with the possibility of optional excursions to Mar del Plata or Buenos Aires.
